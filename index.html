BreakAbout

Based on the original Breakout. I added an extra paddle, a bomb that you need to shoot before it turns red and drops, and two hovering portals. Can you reach 60?

Move and shoot

  • A & D - Move left paddle
  • J & L - Move right paddle
  • W - Shoot left paddle
  • I - Shoot right paddle

Game controls

  • R - Run / Restart
  • P - Pause
  • M - Mute/Unmute music

Easter eggs

  • S - Dance party
  • C - Don't redraw background
